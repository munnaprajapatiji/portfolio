About

I am a first-year PhD scholar in the Department of Mathematics and Computing at IIT (ISM) Dhanbad, working under the supervision of Dr. Schindhiya Laxmi. My long-term goal is a research career in applied mathematics, culminating in a professorship built around one of the directions below.

I hold an MSc in Mathematics from NIT Jamshedpur and a BSc from Dr Shyam Prasad Mukherjee University, Ranchi. My specific PhD focus is still being finalised with my supervisor — this page will be updated as that direction is confirmed.

Scientific AI
